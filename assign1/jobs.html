VMware Architect

Reference Number: 000159

Job Description:

VMware is a Desktop Hypervisor product which lets its users run virtual machines, containers and Kubernetes clusters. VMware virtualizes physical computers using its core hypervisor product.

A hypervisor is a thin layer of software that interacts with the host of the computer, allocates those resources to guest operating systems.

Here, the architects build and design IT strategies, Cloud Configurations including by securing infrastructure solutions for public cloud based environments and other related works creating necessary documentation in support of cloud based technology designs.

Simply they produce virtualization, networking, and security management tools.

At SwinNet, we are hiring for an enthusiastic and preferably skilled VMware specialist to assist our team in managing and maintaining our VMware virtual networks, and in designing new networks for clients.

Salary:

The average salary would be around $100,000 per annum (increases depending on experience).

If you demonstrate exemplar skills in learning and in network skills, you will have opportunities for promotion or bonuses.

Essential Requirements:

This job does not require you to have any experience in specific programming languages.

However, you must know the syntax and basics of the following specific programming languages of C, C++, Java, Python.

You are also required to have at least 3 years of previous job experience in working with VMware or other relevant hypervisor programs

Preferable Skills:

Any previous formal work experience over 3 years in this type of job is highly preferred.

Additionally, further qualifications/eductation in the industry are also preferred, however this depends on the qualification.

Any further understanding/study/qualification in the programming languages C, C++, Java and/or Python is highly preferred.

Main Duties:

This job will involve you:

  • Working with the architecture of VMware EXZi and ESX
  • Maintaining the VMware architecture
  • Providing advice to the SwinNet team on network options and strategies
  • Managing and creating new virtual networks if necessary
